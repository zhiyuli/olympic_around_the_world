What is the purpose of this website?

This website is here to provide some basic information about the Olympics events around the world. We have covered both the Summer and Winter Olympics.

The popups provide information about the the Name of the Host City, Country, Year and the date of Opening Ceremony

We have also provided a detailed route of the torch route of the 2002 Salt Lake City Winter Olympics

We hope that you enjoyed visiting our website today. If you have any questions, comments or suggestions please do let us know.
